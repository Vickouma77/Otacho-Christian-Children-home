Achievements

Our Achievements

100% of the children in our care receive preventative vaccines and necessary medical care, 100% of the children in our care attend school regularly and finish primary and secondary school on time, 100% of the children in our care live in secure and comfortable housing, 100% of the children in our care have sustainable access to clean water and regular nutritious meals. Currently, of the 142 orphans in our care, 51 of the children are receiving early childhood education, 65 are attending primary school, 25 are attending secondary school, and 1 is attending university. Since we opened our doors in 2003, 3 are now adults with stable jobs, 4 youth have graduated from university, and over 47 youth have graduated from secondary school.
